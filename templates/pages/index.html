Originally patterned after the top OEM brands including Mastercraft, Supra, and Moomba. We now offer custom towers for all the top brand Run About boats from 1987 to present.

Every New Dimension wakeboard towers is strong, lightweight, and built to withstand extreme conditions. All of our towers are specifically designed and fabricated to fit your exact make and model of boat without the use of universal joints, the weakness of other aftermarket towers.

Instead of the typical horizontal tube cross-member the New Dimension Tower includes a stylish all aluminum billet plate polished for the optional wakeboard rack forks and accessories to mount.

We use only high quality 1-7/8″ diameter brushed and anodized 6061 aluminum tubing. Creating the most lightweight, and corrosion resistant tower around. Our towers are mounted to the boat with precision machined billet aluminum feet that match the specific profile of your boat removing the need for a large metal plate bolted to the hull, and eliminating a common failure point.
